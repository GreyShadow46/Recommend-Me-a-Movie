Overview

Recommend Me a Movie is a free, non-commercial site dedicated to providing users with high quality movie and tv show recommendations. We take in your preferences to find movies and tv shows that you'll enjoy and may not be aware of. We have 100 movies and shows from 5 different streaming services for you to enjoy. Information on each movie/show is visible on the site such as imdb ratings, where to watch, plot synopsis and trailers etc to help inform your decision.
